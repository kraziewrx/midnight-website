How to Play

Roll for 1, 4, and 24.

Each player rolls six dice. After every roll, at least one die must be kept. To qualify, a player must keep a 1 and a 4. The score is the total of the remaining four dice, with four sixes making the perfect score of 24.

Highest score wins the round. If players tie, the pot carries into the next round.

  1. Roll all six dice.
  2. Keep at least one die after each roll.
  3. Qualify with a 1 and a 4.
  4. Total the remaining four dice.
  5. Highest score wins.
