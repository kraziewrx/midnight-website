Privacy Policy

Midnight Dice Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 15, 2026

We don't collect your data.

Midnight Dice is an offline, pass-and-play dice game. It has no internet connection, no accounts, no analytics, and no advertising. Everything the app knows stays on your device.

What the app stores locally

Midnight Dice saves the following to your device's local storage so your game picks up where you left off:

  • Player names you enter
  • Game progress (current round, scores, dice state)
  • App settings (sound, haptics, shake to roll, house rules)

This data never leaves your device. We cannot see it, access it, or retrieve it. It is deleted when you uninstall the app or use the Clear Saved Game option inside the app.

What we don't do

  • We do not collect personal information
  • We do not use analytics or crash reporting tools
  • We do not serve ads or share data with advertisers
  • We do not sell data to third parties
  • We do not transmit any data to our servers — we don't have any

Children

Midnight Dice does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. It is safe for all ages.

Device Permissions

The app requests access to your device's motion sensor (accelerometer) solely to detect a shake gesture for rolling dice. This data is processed entirely on-device and is never recorded or transmitted.

If this policy changes

If we ever add features that involve data collection — such as online play, leaderboards, analytics, or advertising — this policy will be updated before those features ship, and the "Last updated" date above will reflect the change.
